About me

I am Alisa Rohalska, a Switzerland-based photographer with Ukrainian roots and a lifelong love for capturing real moments. My journey started with film cameras, continued through everyday smartphone storytelling, and today lives in digital photography focused on emotion over perfection.


In a world full of filters and AI-generated images, I choose authenticity: natural textures, gentle color grading in Lightroom, and portraits that preserve who you are instead of turning you into someone else. Life is too short to hide your true self – my work is about celebrating your personality and unique presence.
